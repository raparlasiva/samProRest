A.A. Everywhere

A.A. began on June 10, 1935 in Akron, Ohio

The Fourth Edition of the book "Alcoholics Anonymous" states in the forward, written in 2001, that "worldwide membership of A. A. has just about doubled (since 1976's Third Edition), to an estimated two million or more, with nearly 100,800 groups meeting in approximately 150 countries around the world."
